Organic food and nutrition content

Several studies have compared the nutrition content of organic and conventionally grown plants, and most have shown no significant differences in key vitamin and mineral content. Although the differences are very small, research has shown that some organic food has:

Lower nitrate levels

Higher Vitamin C levels

Higher levels of selenium.

We collect the organic Honey from beehive and process towards to the packaging
