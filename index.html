Organic vs Conventional

Organic Produce
Uses natural fertiliser-manure,compost
Weeds are naturally-controlled using crop rotation, hand weeding, mulching and tilling.
Pests are controlled naturally-derived pesticides
Conventionally-grown produce:
Grown using synthetic or chemical fertilisers
Weeds are controlled with chemical herbicides, commonly glyphosate- the dreaded Roundup
Pests are controlled with synethetic or chemical pesticides
Organic Meat, dairy & eggs
Livestock given organic, hormone-free and GM-free feed
Disease is prevented using natural methods - clean houding, rotational grazing and a healthy diet
Livestock must have access to outdoor areas
Conventionally-reared meat, dairy & eggs
Livestock given growth hormones and non-organic, GM
Antibiotics and medications are routinely employed to prevent disease
Livestock may or may not have access to outdoor areas
